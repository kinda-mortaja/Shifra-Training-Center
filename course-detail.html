UI/UX Fundamentals

Start your journey in UI and UX design — from research to prototype. Hands-on projects, cutting-edge tools, and expert guidance from industry professionals.

Instructor

Ahmed Khalil

Senior Product Designer · 5 years experience

Ahmed worked with startups and large enterprises to build delightful user experiences. He focuses on research-driven design and user-centered interfaces.

About This Course

In this course, you will learn to conduct user research, design clean interfaces, create interactive prototypes, and perform usability tests — all through applied, hands-on exercises.

Curriculum

  • 1. Introduction & Course Overview Preview
  • 2. User Research Methods 4 lectures
  • 3. Information Architecture & Wireframing 5 lectures
  • 4. Visual Design & Component Systems 6 lectures
  • 5. Advanced UI Patterns & Interaction Design 7 lectures
  • 6. Prototyping & Usability Testing 5 lectures

What You'll Learn

  • User Experience (UX) Design principles and fundamentals
  • Designing User Interfaces (UIs) with Figma
  • Transforming user research into testable interfaces
  • Building and evaluating interactive prototypes with real users
